How it works:

  • Search for contracts. Use CoProcure to search for relevant contracts from local agencies, states, and regional and national purchasing cooperatives. Can't find what you're looking for online? Submit a request and we'll respond within 24 hours.
  • Compare contracts and resolve diligence questions. Use CoProcure to compare contracts. Need more information or help with diligence questions? We'll provide free, speedy support in the diligence process.
  • Make a better-informed purchasing decision. You can communicate with vendors on CoProcure. You decide if you'd like to purchase using a contract we've identified. There's no obligation to use a cooperative contract.
