Public procurement collaboration, made easy

Use CoProcure to find and compare cooperative contracts and save public staff time and taxpayer dollars. Leverage the experience of thousands of public agencies nationwide to purchase the solutions you need, from the suppliers you want, in less time.

How it works

  • Search

    Find the most relevant contracts from national and regional purchasing cooperatives, states, and local agencies. Search by keyword, supplier name, contract number, and more.

  • Compare

    Quickly evaluate contracts based on your agency's requirements and other considerations, like supplier type, location, and price.

  • Decide

    Use the most relevant contract to complete your purchase. Track the contracts you're using from other agencies all in one place.

  • Share

    Publish your agency's contracts on CoProcure to promote transparency and help your peers in public service save time, lower costs, and discover new suppliers.
